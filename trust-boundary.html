Doctrine

The model is not the system of record.

Damaros is governed decentralized trial-access infrastructure. AI works upstream on non-PHI protocol intelligence. Patient-path eligibility stays deterministic, evidence-bound, human-reviewed, and replayable across community clinics and network operations. AI upstream. Determinism downstream. Replay always.

01_WHAT_AI_CAN_TOUCH

Permitted AI surface

  • Non-PHI protocol text and sponsor documents.
  • Criterion structuring, ambiguity detection, and mapping suggestions.
  • Governance narration and operator support outside verdict issuance.
  • Policy-bound tasks with provenance and review hooks captured by Luna.
02_WHAT_AI_CANNOT_TOUCH

Prohibited AI surface

  • PHI-bearing patient evidence parsing on the screening path.
  • Eligibility verdict issuance, adjudication, and replay reconstruction.
  • Audit source of truth generation by model output.
  • Any model as system of record for patient-path decisions.
Hard boundary. PHI never touches an LLM on the eligibility path.
03_WHY_THIS_MATTERS

Governance scales clinical AI

Sponsors, CROs, and federal research networks need decisions that reconstruct under scrutiny across sites. A model answer with citations can help discovery. It does not replace a governed execution record.

Regulated AI does not scale through opacity. It scales through replay.

04_HOW_DAMAROS_EXECUTES

Protocol to replay chain

  • Protocol lock: Trident compiles protocol language into executable eligibility structure.
  • Evidence admission: site-approved evidence enters with lineage and timestamps.
  • Deterministic screening: criterion-level PASS, REVIEW, FAIL on the same inputs, same output rule.
  • Human review: REVIEW worklists route blockers and capture adjudication state.
  • Replay: reconstruct version, evidence, criterion outcomes, reviewers, and change history.
Sponsor visibility boundary. Sponsor, CRO, and federal-network views are permissioned and de-identified by default. Site-approved outputs are exposed as execution signals and replay artifacts, not raw PHI payloads.
05_TRIDENT_AND_LUNA

Trident and Luna

  • Trident: compiles protocol language into executable eligibility structure.
  • Luna: records AI origin, policy boundaries, provenance, and execution constraints.
06_THE_FOIL

Respectful category split

Others are validating AI-assisted trial discovery. Damaros focuses on the governed execution layer sponsors, CROs, and research networks need to operationalize and defend those decisions across sites.
07_VALIDATION

Validation dimensions

  • Criterion-level agreement, false PASS, false FAIL.
  • REVIEW burden and missing-evidence drivers.
  • Evidence-lineage completeness and replay completeness.
  • Adjudication deltas and run reproducibility.
Validation covers execution integrity, not clinical efficacy claims.
