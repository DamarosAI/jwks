Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Damaros collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you visit this website or use Damaros services. If you do not agree with this policy, do not use this site or services.

Last updated · May 2026
01_SCOPE

Scope

This policy applies to information collected through damaros.ai and related Damaros services. Separate contractual documents, including a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) or Data Processing Agreement (DPA), may apply for institutional customers. If those agreements conflict with this policy, the signed agreement controls for that customer relationship.

02_INFORMATION_WE_COLLECT

Information we collect

  • Information you provide. Contact details, organization information, and communications you send to us.
  • Service account information. Login identifiers, role assignments, organization scope, and access metadata for authorized users.
  • Usage and device data. IP address, browser type, page views, timestamps, request identifiers, and similar technical telemetry.
  • Customer-provided data. Information processed on behalf of institutional customers, including clinical data when enabled under contract.
03_HOW_WE_USE_INFORMATION

How we use information

We use information to operate, maintain, and improve our services; provide support; authenticate users; secure systems; comply with legal obligations; and communicate with customers and prospective customers.

We may also use data in aggregated or de-identified form for analytics, reporting, and service improvement.

04_HOW_WE_DISCLOSE_INFORMATION

How we disclose information

  • Service providers. We may share information with vendors that host infrastructure, provide analytics, or support operations, subject to contractual confidentiality and security obligations.
  • Customer instructions. For customer data, we act according to the customer contract and documented instructions.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Corporate transactions. Information may be disclosed in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to lawful safeguards.
05_COOKIES_AND_TRACKING

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site functional, remember preferences, and understand site usage. You can manage cookie settings through your browser. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

06_DATA_RETENTION

Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide services, satisfy contractual requirements, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods for customer data may be defined by contract.

07_SECURITY

Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08_HEALTH_INFORMATION

Health information and HIPAA context

When Damaros processes protected health information (PHI) for a covered entity or business associate, that processing is governed by the applicable BAA and customer instructions. In those cases, Damaros operates as a service provider to the customer organization.

Important: Rights requests related to patient records should be directed first to the healthcare institution that controls those records.
09_YOUR_RIGHTS

Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal information, and to object to or request portability of certain processing. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

10_CHILDREN

Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through this site.

11_INTERNATIONAL_TRANSFERS

International data transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate transfer safeguards under applicable law.

12_POLICY_CHANGES

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page. Material changes may be communicated through additional notice where required by law.
