Execution Spine

Protocol to proof. One governed run.

Protocol

Intent becomes logic.

Sponsor criteria, windows, and evidence rules compile once, then deploy identically to every site.

Evidence

Records become admissible.

Site-approved data arrives with source lineage, freshness, and mapping, ready to screen against.

Screening

A verdict, not a guess.

Every criterion runs against locked logic. Anything unclear becomes REVIEW, never a fabricated answer.

Replay

Execution becomes proof.

Any decision rebuilds on demand: protocol, evidence, screening, REVIEW, and who authorized it.

No LLM verdicts PHI-bounded Replayable proof

Operations Mesh

The machinery behind governed trial execution.

Trident

Makes protocols executable.

Trial rules become usable logic.

Trident turns criteria, timelines, evidence needs, and ambiguity into clear instructions every site can run the same way.

Luna

Makes AI inspectable.

Every AI step is governed and recorded.

Luna tracks each suggestion, task, handoff, and output against policy, PHI limits, source evidence, human REVIEW, and Replay.

Forge

Builds the work packet.

Scattered records become trial-ready evidence.

Forge assembles patient context, source links, missing data, and criterion support so humans can act faster.

Router

Moves work to the right decision.

Nothing ambiguous gets buried.

Router sends gaps, conflicts, exceptions, and judgment calls to the right human or deterministic path.
