Damaros

Damaros is the execution OS for clinical trials inside the hospital. It compiles complex eligibility, resolves fragmented evidence, and screens every patient deterministically. AI-native infrastructure for the future of patient care.

The mission

Run trials at the pace of technology.

Trial execution is still largely manual. Eligibility lives in dense protocols, evidence is spread across separate systems, and review happens chart by chart, criterion by criterion.

~80%of trials miss their enrollment timelines
1 in 5activated sites enroll zero patients
<5%of eligible patients are ever approached

As trial complexity, coordinator burden, and the pace of drug discovery accelerate, these gaps compound. Damaros is the substrate that scales execution to keep up, so a hospital can run trials at the pace of the technology driving them.
