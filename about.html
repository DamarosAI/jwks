About Damaros™

Care happens everywhere. The trials that save lives don't.

One mission: get more patients to therapeutic options faster

Why now

AI doesn't fix the bottleneck. It makes it worse.

AI is accelerating discovery, more candidates, more protocols, more amendments, tighter eligibility. None of it reaches a patient until a site executes. The constraint was never discovery. It is execution, and every model upstream multiplies the work downstream.

At major centers

More open protocols per coordinator, more amendments to re-screen against. Throughput stalls on the same manual eligibility work, just more of it.

At community sites

The patients are already there, but without an execution layer the matches are never seen in time. The gap between care and trial only widens.

More intelligence in the pipeline makes a deterministic, replayable execution layer more necessary, not less.

From the founder

My goal is to bring trials to the community clinics where most patients are already treated, on an execution platform that scales with any site size.

Anirudh C. Nimmagadda
