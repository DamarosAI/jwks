Validation

Built for validation before deployment.

Validation in Damaros is staged, inspectable, and tied to the same execution chain used in production across sites. This is how sponsors, CROs, and federal networks trust deterministic screening and replay at scale.

Honesty callout. This page describes engineering and execution validation only. It does not claim clinical efficacy, FDA clearance, or sponsor endorsement.
01_ENGINEERING_VALIDATION

Engineering validation

Current matrix: 100 synthetic patients across 6 NCT-anchored protocol locks and 9 ingestion modalities, totaling 5,400 deterministic evaluation slots.

02_RETROSPECTIVE_VALIDATION

Retrospective validation

Closed cohorts with adjudicated outcomes are replayed to measure agreement, review burden, site bottlenecks, and evidence quality before any prospective network rollout.
